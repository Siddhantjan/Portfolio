Competitive programming or Project

I was thinking that if you have a star on harkerrank,codeshef or many practice sites or you have a project then any company can hire you easily. but when I met a person who gets a job in IBM and he gives me advice that you have the programming skills and also projects, internships then you will get a job. but you have to clear the written exam so to qualify the written exam you have some knowledge of data structure, algorithms. so I personally feel that if you don't have any projects but you familiar with competitive programming then you easily qualify the written exam and get that job.

but I am not saying that you will not make projects actually both things are connected you know to write code it means your programming skills are better and once you know how to write then you easily make projects.

so you brush up your coding skills through competitive programming as well as you have to make projects because it gives you to show what you learn.

Both are important
